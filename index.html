About

Amir Hossein Kargaran is a research assistant in the Department of Communications and Networking at Aalto University. Amir Hossein Kargaran is a research assistant in the Department of Communications and Networking at Aalto University. He works at User Interfaces Research Group led by Antti Oulasvirta. He is also a M.Sc. student in the Department of Computer Engineering at Sharif University of Technology (SUT) and a member of the Automated Software Engineering (ASE) research laboratory led by Prof. Abbas Heydarnoori.

Prior to that, in reverse chronological order, He was a member of Izadi's research group led by Prof. Iman Izadi, and a member of the Game Theory and Mechanism Design (GTMD) research laboratory and Institute for Artificial Intelligence (IAI) led by Prof. Mohammad Hossein Manshaei at the Isfahan University of Technology (IUT), Iran.

He obtained two B.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Engineering from the Isfahan University of Technology (IUT), Iran in 2020.
