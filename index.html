About Me

I am a M.Sc. student at Department of Computer Engineering, Sharif University of Technology (SUT). Currently, I am a member of the Automated Software Engineering (ASE) research laboratory under the supervision of Prof. Abbas Heydarnoori.

Previosly, in reverse chronological order, I was a member of Izadi's research group led by Prof. Iman Izadi, and a member of the Game Theory and Mechanism Design (GTMD) research laboratory and Institute for Artificial Intelligence (IAI) led by Prof. Mohammad Hossein Manshaei at the Isfahan University of Technology (IUT), Iran, where I graduated with two B.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Engineering.
